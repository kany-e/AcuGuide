# Safety Copy

## Product Safety Position

AcuGuide is a wellness self-care coach. It does not diagnose, treat, prescribe, or replace medical care.

中文：

AcuGuide 是 wellness 自我护理教练，不做诊断、不治疗、不开处方，也不替代专业医疗建议。

## Safety Screen

### English

AcuGuide provides wellness self-care guidance only. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace medical care. Stop if you feel sharp pain, numbness, dizziness, unusual symptoms, or worsening discomfort. Seek medical advice for severe, sudden, persistent, or concerning symptoms.

### 中文

AcuGuide 仅提供 wellness 自我护理指导，不做诊断，也不替代专业医疗建议。如果出现刺痛、麻木、眩晕、异常症状或不适加重，请停止练习。严重、突然、持续或令人担心的症状应寻求专业帮助。

## Short In-App Safety Copy

- This is a self-care routine, not medical diagnosis.
- Use gentle pressure only.
- Stop if you feel sharp pain, numbness, dizziness, or worsening discomfort.
- Do not press on broken, irritated, swollen, or infected skin.
- If pregnant or possibly pregnant, avoid certain pressure points unless advised by a clinician.

## Red Flag Stop Copy

### English

Your symptoms may need more than a self-care routine. Please stop this exercise and consider seeking medical advice, especially if symptoms are severe, sudden, persistent, or worsening.

### 中文

你的情况可能不适合继续自我护理练习。请先停止按压；如果症状严重、突然、持续或加重，建议寻求专业帮助。

## Forbidden Claims

不要在 UI、pitch、Devpost 使用：

- 治疗经痛
- 治疗头痛
- 诊断身体问题
- 检测疾病
- 判断体质
- 找出疼痛原因
- 按这里可以治好
- AI 中医医生

## Allowed Claims

可以使用：

- guided self-care
- wellness support
- comfort routine
- educational acupressure guidance
- camera-based technique feedback
- real-time guidance for hand position and hold duration

## Safety Checklist

- [ ] 每条 routine 前有 safety copy。
- [ ] 每个高风险入口都有 stop condition。
- [ ] Recap 不写 “symptom resolved”。
- [ ] Devpost 不写 treatment claim。
- [ ] Pitch 里主动说 not an AI doctor。
