# Demo Video Shot List

## Target Length

60-90 seconds.

## Video Structure

### Shot 1: Problem

Duration: 5-8 seconds

Screen:

- Simple text or app home screen.

Voiceover:

> Acupressure advice is easy to find online, but users still do not know whether they are doing it correctly.

### Shot 2: Product Intro

Duration: 8-10 seconds

Screen:

- AcuGuide home with three symptom entries.

Voiceover:

> AcuGuide is an AI camera coach for safe, guided hand acupressure routines.

### Shot 3: Select Tension Headache

Duration: 5 seconds

Screen:

- Click Tension Headache.

Voiceover:

> For the demo, we choose a tension headache routine.

### Shot 4: Safety Boundary

Duration: 6-8 seconds

Screen:

- Safety screen.

Voiceover:

> Before starting, AcuGuide makes the safety boundary clear: this is wellness self-care, not medical diagnosis.

### Shot 5: Routine Preview

Duration: 8 seconds

Screen:

- 30-second routine preview.

Voiceover:

> The routine content is curated, so the AI does not invent medical advice.

### Shot 6: Camera Feedback

Duration: 20-25 seconds

Screen:

- no hand
- hand detected
- target alignment
- good position
- progress timer

Voiceover:

> The camera feedback loop checks whether the hand is visible, whether the finger is near the target region, and whether the user holds steady long enough.

### Shot 7: Recap

Duration: 8 seconds

Screen:

- completion recap.

Voiceover:

> The recap summarizes execution quality, not medical outcome.

### Shot 8: Closing

Duration: 5 seconds

Screen:

- Product name / tagline.

Voiceover:

> AcuGuide moves AI health from advice generation to action guidance.

## Capture Checklist

- [ ] Record at least one clean golden path.
- [ ] Record a backup mock mode path.
- [ ] Cursor movements are slow and visible.
- [ ] UI text is readable.
- [ ] No private API keys or terminal windows visible.
- [ ] No treatment or diagnosis claims appear on screen.

## Video Description

AcuGuide is an AI camera coach that helps users perform safe hand acupressure routines. The demo shows a tension headache routine with safety boundaries, camera-based hand feedback, target alignment, hold timing, and a non-diagnostic completion recap.
