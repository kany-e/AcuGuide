## Current Product Stance

Use this wording everywhere:

> AcuGuide is an AI camera coach for safe, guided hand acupressure routines.

Avoid this wording everywhere:

> AI doctor, diagnosis, treatment, disease detection, cure, detects what is wrong with your body.

## Golden Demo Path

Tension Headache -> Safety -> Routine Preview -> Camera Feedback -> Completion Recap

Secondary entries can exist for Period Discomfort and Neck & Shoulder Tension, but the full live camera demo should prioritize Tension Headache.
