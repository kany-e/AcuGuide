# Slide Outline

## Slide 1: Title

**AcuGuide Hand Coach**  
AI camera coach for safe, guided hand acupressure routines.

Visual:

- App home screen or camera overlay screenshot.

Speaker line:

> Static acupressure advice tells you where to press. AcuGuide shows you whether you are doing it right.

## Slide 2: Problem

Title:

**The gap is execution, not information.**

Bullets:

- Articles and diagrams are static.
- Videos cannot adapt to the user's hand position.
- Chatbots can explain, but cannot see the action.
- Users need confidence that they are doing the routine correctly and safely.

Visual:

- Comparison: article/video/chatbot vs. camera-guided feedback.

## Slide 3: Product

Title:

**A camera-guided self-care routine.**

Flow:

1. Choose discomfort.
2. Review safety boundary.
3. Follow guided hand routine.
4. Get real-time camera feedback.
5. Complete non-diagnostic recap.

Visual:

- 5-step product flow.

## Slide 4: AI And Safety

Title:

**Constrained AI, visible feedback, clear boundaries.**

Bullets:

- Curated routine library as source of truth.
- Vision feedback for hand visibility and target alignment.
- AI coach language for step-by-step guidance.
- Red-flag safety responses.
- Not diagnosis, not treatment.

Visual:

- Three-layer architecture: Routine Data -> Camera Feedback -> AI Coach.

## Slide 5: Demo And Future

Title:

**From advice generation to action guidance.**

Bullets:

- Demo: Tension Headache hand routine.
- Future: expert-reviewed routine library, voice guidance, better calibration, more guided self-care flows.

Closing:

> We are not building another health chatbot. We are building the missing execution layer for safe, guided self-care routines.
