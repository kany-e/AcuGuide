# Pitch Script

## 15-Second Pitch

AcuGuide is an AI camera coach for hand acupressure. It helps users follow safe self-care routines by showing whether their hand is visible, whether they are pressing near the target area, and whether they are holding long enough.

## 30-Second Pitch

People can find acupressure advice online, but they often cannot tell whether they are pressing the right place. AcuGuide turns static advice into a camera-guided self-care routine. It gives real-time feedback on hand position, target alignment, stability, and duration, while keeping clear safety boundaries so it stays wellness guidance, not medical diagnosis.

## 90-Second Demo Pitch

People can find acupressure advice everywhere online. The hard part is not reading the advice; the hard part is knowing whether you are pressing the right place, holding long enough, and doing it safely.

AcuGuide is an AI camera coach for guided hand acupressure routines. It is not an AI doctor. It does not diagnose or treat. It helps users execute safe self-care routines with real-time visual feedback.

For this demo, I will choose **Tension Headache**. Before the routine starts, AcuGuide shows a safety boundary: this is wellness guidance, not medical care, and users should stop if symptoms feel sharp, unusual, or worsening.

Now the user enters the camera-guided routine. The app checks whether the hand is visible, overlays an approximate target region, and gives feedback. If the hand is out of frame, it asks the user to move into frame. If the finger is off target, it asks the user to adjust. Once the finger is near the target region and stable, the timer starts.

At the end, the recap summarizes execution quality: hold time, stability, and completion. It does not claim the headache was treated. If the user reports worsening or unusual discomfort, AcuGuide tells them to stop rather than keep pressing.

We are not building another health chatbot. We are building the missing execution layer for safe, guided self-care routines.

## Chinese Pitch

AcuGuide 不是 AI 医生，而是手部穴位按压的 AI 摄像头教练。

网上有很多穴位建议，但用户真正的问题不是找不到信息，而是不知道自己有没有按对、按多久、动作是否稳定、什么时候应该停。AcuGuide 把静态建议变成一个可以跟着做的 routine：用户选择常见不适，先看到安全提示，再通过摄像头完成手部按压。系统会反馈手是否入镜、手指是否接近目标区域、是否保持稳定，以及是否完成足够时长。

我们的价值不是让 AI 诊断疾病，而是让 AI 帮用户更安全、更确定地执行一个自我护理动作。

## Closing Line Options

- We are moving AI health from advice generation to action guidance.
- Static advice tells users what to do. AcuGuide helps them do it correctly.
- This is not an AI doctor. It is a visual execution layer for self-care.
